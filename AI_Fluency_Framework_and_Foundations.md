## AI Fluency: Framework and Foundations 

summary of the AI Fluency Framework and its core competencies:

- AI Fluency means working with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely.
- The framework is built around four main skills called "The 4 Ds": Design, Description, Development, Deployment.
- Delegation is about deciding what tasks AI should do and what humans should handle.
- "Problem Awareness" in Delegation means knowing your goals and what needs to be done before using AI.
- Description is about clearly telling AI what you want, how you want it done, and how you want to interact.
- "Product Description" means specifying exactly what you want AI to create or provide.
- Discernment is about critically checking AI’s outputs, processes, and behaviors for quality and appropriateness.
- "Process Discernment" means assessing if AI’s reasoning and problem-solving are effective.
- Diligence focuses on responsible AI use, transparency, and accountability.
- "Transparency Diligence" means being open and honest about AI’s role in your work with those who need to know.
